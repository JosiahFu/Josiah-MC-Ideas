Soul fire blaze

Has 40 HP
Spawns very rarely in the soul sand valley, and also when a skeleton walks into soul fire or any skeleton skull is dropped into soul fire
Has the skull of a skeleton, but with soul fire inside
Has shields instead of rods
Shoots soul fireballs (twice damage)
shoots 10 fireballs in 1 second, then cooldown for 5 seconds
shields have a 50% chance of blocking any attacks
If there are players within 2 blocks, it charges for 5 seconds and releases a shockwave attack (knockback+damage)
50% chance of dropping a Seared Skeleton Skull
100% chance of dropping a blaze shield
Extinguishes after 2 minutes, leaving behind a wither skeleton
Skull blackens over 2 minutes
