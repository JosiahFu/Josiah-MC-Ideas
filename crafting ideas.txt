==Gilded Armor==
{{Smithing Table
|Input1=Diamond Chestplate
|Input2=Gold Ingot,8
|Output=[Gilded Diamond Chestplate]Golden Chestplate
}}
* Will make piglins neutral to you
* Has the same durability as an equivalent gold armor
* Keeps name and enchantments
* When the durability runs out, the original piece of armor is returned
* If a piglin picks it up and inspects it, the piglin will realize it is not solid gold and it will no longer protect you from piglins
