Petrified Woods

Only spawns creepers
Trees are made of petrified logs and leaves
Ground is made of gravel with moss on top
Patches of ash and magma

==Petrified Logs==
Decorative building block
Can be polished
Cannot be crafted into planks

==Petrified Leaves==
Decorative building block
Gives gravel when broken (unless with silk touch)

==Moss==
New plant
Is 1 pixel high, like carpet
grows on stone type blocks
grows quickly on gravel

==Ash==
New block
Falls like sand
Created sometimes when blocks are burned or blown up
Explosions, fire, lava, and magma blocks "heat" ash
Hot ash does not fall and inflicts damage like magma blocks
